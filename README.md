# WhatToEat

Recommending different recipes with a detailed description, based on what ingredient you would like to eat and what are your preferences.

## Preferencess

* Lactose free
* Sugar free
* Alcohol free
* Gluten free
* Vegetarian
* Vegan
* Kosher
